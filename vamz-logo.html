Naming, Logo Design

Brand Story

Vamz is a dropshipping business based in the United Kingdom that promotes and sells active lifestyle products.

Adaptable but Identifiable Identity for VAMZ

The project is about crafting a logo for a company which promotes active wear & accessories. For the name, we wanted to create a name that represents the brand's core that blends with the name of the founder 'VAMSI'. Word which that represents the active founders lifestyle Agile, and Active, VAMZ best reflected the key features of the brand: movement, performance, and energy. After that, the objective was to create distinctive and memorable brand visuals using the minimalistic approach.

Challenge

Vamzi contacted me to help them get rid of their old and complicated logo and replace it with something fresh and special that better represents them and their work in the active wear industry. In order to generate more leads, they also wanted to update their brand and their visibility through various social media channels.

Strategy

Working in the active lifestyle industry, 'VAMZ' had an outdated logo that didn't accurately represent their brand. However, in this new age and modern sports industry, where everyone is focused on modern and bold design trends to draw more and more clients, it was a challenge for both of us to fully adapt while still obtaining something that works for 'VAMZ' and what they provide to the industry, namely, agile and bold.

Logotype

What I did in the first phase of this project is the research of the company's competitors to highlight uniqueness and diffrentiate and my main goal was to understand what works and what's useless and this research allowed me to create something brand new.

Solution

As a result of this research I combined all the strategies and attributes that I evaluated and VAMZ wanted to implement on the minds of it's clients to get the better feeling of this active lifestyle brand i.e., electric, energetic, modern and bold.
