Landing page design

Target Audience

Luxury vacations for couples in their fifties with discretionary income.

Tools

Figma, Photoshop

Work Duration

2 weeks

Key Findings

The Challenge

Metro Travel, a tour operator with over 20 years of experience in the travel industry, is recognised for its in-depth knowledge of its destinations and expertise in creating truly customised journeys.

Approach

A lot of pictures would be included. To make the photos pop, use light, beachy colours. Metro needed a dazzling landing page that could display the best of the world's offerings.

Conclusion

Metro Travel has been able to showcase their journeys in greater detail thanks to stunning imagery and design, as well as simple navigation of their tour packages, dramatically increasing customer and employee satisfaction.

Results

The updated Metro Travel landing page layout resulted in a 32 percent rise in conversions.
